Marathon Women

Today it’s hardly to understand that women were not allowed to participate in marathon events 50 years ago. Only by the tenacity of the first female marathon pioneers it was possible to tear down the wall. The data research and the visualization shows the increase in female participants in the most popular marathon in the world – The New York City Marathon. The increase is linked to the historical issue of women in marathons and collected to the actual status of female participants in the today 6 most important marathons of the world. Women Catching Up – Yes! But in different ways.
